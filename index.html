In the span of only a few years, R has become the lingua franca of statistics. The Titanic Introduction To R (TITR) Workshops are a series of three hour cruises designed to introduce new users to R.

In order to make these workshops useful to a wide variety of participants, pre-requisites are almost non-existent. Participants are not expected to have prior programming experience, but they are expected to have a basic understanding of the statistical concepts discussed during the workshop they are taking.

To learn more about the workshops, visit the Workshops page.
